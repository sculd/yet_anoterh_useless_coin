Bonus tokens!

Every block has a 1 in 256 chance of issuing bonus tokens.

When purchasing YUC, the smart contract will take a hash of the current block's coinbase, number, and timestamp. If the first byte of the hash is 0x00, the block becomes a bonus block. During a bonus block, the smart contract counts the number of bits set in the second byte of the hash and uses that number as a bonus multiplier.

Every YUC purchase of at least 0.01 ETH worth during the bonus block will receive, in addition to the base distribution, an additional 100 YUC per Ether multiplied by the bonus multiplier. That means that contributors have a chance of receiving a total of nine times the number of tokens they expected!
